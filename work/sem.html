A Semi-Empirical model for galaxies

A research project in which we generated mock catalogs of galaxies and studied the drivers of their statistical properties.

Python
Pandas
Bootstrap
Flat tables
RegEx

This is the second project from my PhD thesis and follows a complementary approach to the work in my first project where we used observational data.

In this project we simulated astronomical sources, creating mock catalogs (i.e. flat tables), by using empirical laws that rule their properties, looking for insights into what drove the statistical relations among galaxy physical properties found in the literature.

Here is a 5 min talk I gave at a virtual conference with preliminary results. See my first author paper (journal and arXiv), my PhD thesis (Chapter 3) and its GitHub repository.
