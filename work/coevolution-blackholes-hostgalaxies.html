Co-evolution of black holes and host galaxies

My first published work, studying a large sample of galaxies

IDL
Flat tables
Bootstrap
Set operations

This is the first project from my PhD thesis, where we performed a statistical study on a large sample of galaxies.

I worked mostly with flat tables containing astronomical sources, I cross-matched the tables and selected sources based on their physical properties, we performed a stacking analysis on their images to extract average statistics and confidence ranges for my samples of sources.

For this project I worked in IDL, a quite common programming language in astronomy.

See my first author paper here, or Chapter 2 of my thesis.
