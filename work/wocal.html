WO Cal

A calendar to keep track of your workouts following your favorite YouTube videos.

Python
Django

During the lockdown I started working out at home, and just like many people, I found plenty of workout inspirations on YouTube.

But that alone wasn't enough. I wanted to keep track of what I did every day, and needed to answer questions like: how often did I workout last week? Am I following this favorite video of mine too often? Have I done any abs workout this week?

I looked for a solution to my problem but I couldn't find any.

So I ended up making my own Django App, which relies on Python and a SQLite database.

Here I can save all my favorite videos in a single place, classify them with tags, watch them, look for that HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) that makes me sweat so much, make sure I rotate through all those core workouts, etc.

Furthermore, I have all that calories/heartbeat/steps info from my smart band, so I made sure I could collect that info as well.

See my BitBucket repository.
