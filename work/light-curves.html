Light curves

A research project studying the variation of active black holes at the centers of galaxies

Python
Pandas
Bokeh
Time series

I extracted light curves (time series) of variable sources from their raw images. I cross-correlated light curves with different physical origins and I simulated them, in order to retrieve additional constraints on their physical properties.

Part of this work was published here (see section ...). (light curves notebook, plots, thesis project) and the rest can be seen in this notebook.
